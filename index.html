MEMORY CARD GAME


Welcome to the Memory Card Game


Test your memory and challenge your friends!


Rules


  • This is a two-player game.
  • The goal is to find all the matching pairs of cards.
  • Players take turns flipping two cards at a time.
  • If the two cards match, the player scores a point and gets another turn.
  • If the two cards don't match, the cards are flipped back, and the turn passes to the other player.
  • The game ends when all pairs are matched.
  • The player with the most points at the end wins!

Instructions


  1. Click on the "Start Game" button to begin.
  2. On your turn, click two cards to flip them over and reveal their images.
  3. If the images match, they will stay flipped, and you earn a point.
  4. If the images don’t match, they will flip back, and it’s the other player’s turn.
  5. Keep playing until all pairs are found!
  6. View the score and turn updates on the screen.

Good Luck and Have Fun!
